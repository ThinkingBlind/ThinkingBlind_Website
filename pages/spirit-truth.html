The Spirit of Truth

The spirit of truth doesn't just dump ansers on you. It walks with you while you untangle lies you didn't even know you believed.

Marks of the Spirit of Truth

  • Clarity that survives hard questions.
  • Conviction without cruelty.
  • Peace that doesn't require pretending.
  • Correction that leads to growth, not shame.

Ways to Lean Into Truth

  1. Pray or relfect honestly about what you're afraid to lose if you're wrong.
  2. Search the scriptures and real supporting-evidence instead of just hunting for ammo.
  3. Practice saying "I don't know yet, but I'm willing to confirm."
  4. Let new understanding change how you actually move, not just how you talk.
