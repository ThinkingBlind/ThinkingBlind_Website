The Spirit of Error

The spirit of error isn't just "being wrong." It's the pattern of protecting a lie because it feels safer than facing the truth.

How the Spirit of Error Shows Up

  • Needing to win arguments more than undertand anything.
  • Weapoinizing scripture, logic, or "research" to avoid self-examination.
  • Projecting your fears onto everyone else as if they're the threat.
  • Cofusing stubbornness with faitfulness.

Steps to Disarm the Spirit of Error

  1. Admit that you could be wrong, even about "sacred" opinions.
  2. Ask somone you trustto challenge your assumptions honestly.
  3. Track the moments you feel exposed and notice the patterns.
  4. Choose alignment with truth over comfort, one small decision at a time.
